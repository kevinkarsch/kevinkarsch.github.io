I am co-founder and CTO at Lightform where I lead the technical and research divisions of the company. We've created the first computer for projected AR (LF1), the first integrated projector for projected AR (LF2), and Lightform Creator, a powerful yet easy-to-use projected AR software. Lightform has also produced Lowe's Canvas, an auto-calibrating projection system for home redecoration, installed at select Lowe's locations across the US. My work revolves around the intersection of computer vision and computer graphics, usually involving machine learning and MR/AR/VR. I'm also an experienced cross-platform desktop software engineer (C++, python), with some embedded systems knowledge. Prior to Lightform, I spent time at Adobe Research, Microsoft Research, and a few other computer vision focused startups. I completed my PhD at UIUC advised by David Forsyth and Derek Hoiem.
