Kevin Karsch

cv | google scholar | patents | arxiv | vimeo | github | linkedin

I'm co-founder and CTO at Lightform (formerly Lumenous). We're making both hardware and software for projection mapping, which is just another way to say "projecting displays, information, or graphics on ordinary objects." It's shared augmented reality, and best of all, you don't need to wear anything. I completed my PhD at UIUC, advised by David Forsyth and Derek Hoiem, and my research interests lie at the intersection of computer vision and computer graphics. At Lightform, we're commercializing computer vision-inspired research projects to develop an easy to use, commercial-grade projection mapping platform.
